About Me

I am a Computer Science student at Western University, and I have the ability to design and build reliable software systems. My skills include C/C++, Python, Java, and JavaScript, with particular strengths in systems programming, firmware development, and collaborative software engineering. With this foundation, I create efficient low-level solutions as well as scalable applications that address modern software challenges.

In addition to software engineering, I bring strong skills in game development and 3D art. Using Blender and Godot, I can design interactive 3D experiences, develop shaders, and create cinematic renderings — blending technical expertise with creative problem-solving. With fluency in English, Japanese, Traditional Chinese, and Simplified Chinese, I collaborate effectively across diverse teams. I am currently open to internships and new graduate opportunities where I can contribute to building reliable, scalable, and visually engaging software.
